-Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)-

A. What is Affor-Da-Drive?

Affor-Da-Drive Fare Guide is a website that makes booking or using public transport quicker and easier. Our webstite can keep you updated on the transport schedules and current rates. Additionally, we offer discounts to undergraduates, to senior citizens, and to our country's military personnel who opt to use our website to directly book their transport.

C. Who Are The Individuals Legible for Discounts?

Fare discounts on our websites are typically reserved only for the following:

  • Undergrad Students
  • Philippines Military Personnel
  • Senior Citizens

The discounts range from 15%/25%/30% correspondingly. But they can also be obtained by exchanging "Affor-Da-Points" that you obtain by booking on our website. Currently as of 2026, only one discount can be applied per booking.
