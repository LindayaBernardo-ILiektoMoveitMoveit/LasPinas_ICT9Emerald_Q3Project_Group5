-Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)-

A. What is Affor-Da-Drive?

Affor-Da-Drive Fare Guide is a website that makes booking or using public transport quicker and easier. Our webstite can keep you updated on the transport schedules and current rates. Additionally, you can book metro rail transit rides directly from our website. We also offer discounts to undergraduates, to senior citizens, to PWD, and to our country's military personnel who opt to use our website to directly book their transport.

B. How Do I Book A Ride?

  1. Click the 'Book Now' button on the navigation bar...
  2. Fill out the following information (e.g., Pick-Up and Drop-Off Locations)
  3. Click "Calculate Fare" and let us calculate your toral!
  • Bonus: You can apply for discounts under the "Discount" tab

C. Who Are The Individuals Legible for Discounts?

Fare discounts on our websites are typically reserved only for the following:

  • Undergrad Students
  • Philippines Military Personnel
  • Senior Citizens
    • PWD

The discounts typically take out 20% off the initial total fare. But they can also be obtained by exchanging "Affor-Da-Points" that you obtain by booking on our website: a new feature added to our website to make prices more afforable.

D. How Can I Apply Discounts on the Fare?

Click the 'Discounts' tab and click whatever applies (e.g., Undergraduate Student, Philippine Military, Senior Citizen, PWD). If you wish to exchange points for discounts, click "Affor-Da-Point Exchange" and input the amount...
